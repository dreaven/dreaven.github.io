Introduction

Hello, welcome to my homepage. I am a senior software engineer working on large-scale machine learning at Google AI, New York. I received B.S. in Electronic Engineering (2009) from Tsinghua University (admitted to Tsinghua Univeristy as the Champion of National College Entrance Exam in Anhui Province in 2005), and M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering (2011), M.S. in Mathematics (2012), and Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering (2013) from University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. My PhD thesis is The Optimal Mechanism in Differential Privacy. My work has been selected as the cover photo of IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Signal Processing. I have had the privilege of working with Professor Pramod Viswanath as a research assistant at Coordinated Science Laboratory.
